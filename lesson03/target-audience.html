Target Audience:

  • Who:

    Local business owners trying to improve the quality of life of those in their community and promote a healthy economy

  • Age:

    16-100

  • Income:

    Income levels will very between business owners. Typically above 40,000 a year.

  • Motivation:

    Desire to promote healthy economic growth, partner with others, create job opportunities, and create good public relations.
